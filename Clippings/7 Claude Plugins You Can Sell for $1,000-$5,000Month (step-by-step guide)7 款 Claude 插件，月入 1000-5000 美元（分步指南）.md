# Part 1: 7 Plugins You Could Build and Sell第一部分：你可以开发和销售的 7 种插件

## 1\. Client Onboarding Plugin1. 客户入职插件

**What it does:** Walks a new client through a structured intake process. Asks the right questions, generates a project brief, creates deliverable timelines, and saves everything to a shared folder.**功能：** 引导新客户完成结构化的需求收集流程。提出正确的问题，生成项目简报，创建交付时间表，并将所有内容保存到共享文件夹中。

**Who buys it:** Agencies, consultants, freelancers who onboard 5+ clients per month.**目标客户：** 每月新增 5 个以上客户的代理机构、顾问和自由职业者。

**How to price it:** $500-1,500 one-time setup + $200/month maintenance.**定价方式：** 一次性安装费 500-1500 美元 + 每月维护费 200 美元。

**Why it works:** Every agency has an onboarding process. Most of them are messy. This standardizes it and saves 2-3 hours per client.**其有效性：** 每家代理机构都有客户导入流程，但大多数流程都很混乱。这种方法可以规范流程，并为每位客户节省 2-3 小时。

## 2\. Weekly Report Generator Plugin2. 周报生成器插件

**What it does:** Pulls data from connected tools (Google Sheets, project management platforms, email), summarizes key metrics, and generates a formatted weekly report ready to send to clients or stakeholders.**它的功能：** 从连接的工具（Google Sheets、项目管理平台、电子邮件）中提取数据，汇总关键指标，并生成格式化的每周报告，以便发送给客户或利益相关者。

**Who buys it:** Agency owners, consultants who send regular client updates, operations managers.**购买者：** 代理机构老板、定期向客户发送更新信息的顾问、运营经理。

**How to price it:** $1,000-2,500 setup + $300/month.**定价方式：** 设置费 1,000-2,500 美元 + 每月 300 美元。

**Why it works:** Nobody likes writing weekly reports. Everyone needs them. This plugin turns a 2-hour task into a 2-minute review.**它的作用原理：** 没人喜欢写周报，但每个人都需要写。这款插件能把原本需要 2 小时才能完成的任务缩短到 2 分钟。

## 3\. Content Repurposing Plugin3. 内容重用插件

**What it does:** Takes one piece of long-form content (podcast transcript, blog post, YouTube video) and generates 10+ pieces of short-form content: tweets, LinkedIn posts, email newsletters, Instagram captions, all in the user's voice.**它的功能：** 将一篇长篇内容（播客文字稿、博客文章、YouTube 视频）生成 10 多篇短篇内容：推文、LinkedIn 帖子、电子邮件简报、Instagram 标题，全部以用户的口吻撰写。

**Who buys it:** Creators, coaches, solopreneurs who publish content regularly.**目标用户：** 内容创作者、教练、定期发布内容的个体经营者。

**How to price it:** $1,500-3,000 setup + $500/month.**定价方式：** 设置费 1,500-3,000 美元 + 每月 500 美元。

**Why it works:** Content repurposing is the #1 bottleneck for creators. They have the content, they just don't have time to chop it up. This does it in minutes.**其原理：** 内容再利用是创作者面临的最大瓶颈。他们拥有内容，只是没有时间进行剪辑。而这个工具只需几分钟就能完成。

## 4\. Client Renewal & Upsell Intelligence Plugin 4. 客户续订和追加销售智能插件

**What it does:** Monitors your existing client base (contracts, invoices, communication history, project timelines) and flags when a client is approaching renewal, hasn't been contacted in X days, or has signals they're ready for an upsell. Generates a personalized re-engagement message or upsell pitch based on their history with you. **功能：** 监控您现有的客户群（合同、发票、沟通记录、项目时间表），并在客户即将续约、超过 X 天未联系或出现可能准备追加销售的迹象时发出警报。根据客户的历史记录，生成个性化的重新联系信息或追加销售方案。

**Who buys it:** Agencies, consultants, coaches, anyone with recurring clients. **谁会购买它：** 代理机构、顾问、教练，以及任何拥有回头客的人。

**How to price it:** $2,000-3,000 setup + $400/month. **定价方式：** 设置费 2,000-3,000 美元 + 每月 400 美元。

**Why it works:** Most service businesses lose revenue from forgetting to follow up. This plugin analyzes your specific client relationships, your services, and your pricing tiers and tells you what to do next.**工作原理：** 大多数服务型企业因忘记跟进客户而损失收入。这款插件会分析您特定的客户关系、您的服务和您的定价体系，并告诉您下一步该怎么做。

## 5\. Proposal/SOW Generator Plugin5. 提案/工作说明书生成器插件

**What it does:** Based on a short conversation or intake form, generates a professionally formatted proposal or statement of work. Includes scope, timeline, pricing, terms, and deliverables.**功能：** 根据简短的对话或需求收集表，生成格式专业的提案或工作说明书。内容包括范围、时间表、定价、条款和交付成果。

**Who buys it:** Freelancers, agencies, consultants who write 3+ proposals per month.**谁会购买它：** 每月撰写 3 份以上提案的自由职业者、代理机构和顾问。

**How to price it:** $1,500-2,500 setup + $300/month.**定价方式：** 设置费 1500-2500 美元 + 每月 300 美元。

**Why it works:** Proposals take hours to write. Most of the content is repetitive across clients. This cuts proposal creation from hours to minutes and keeps quality consistent.**其优势在于：** 撰写提案通常需要数小时，而且大多数内容在不同客户之间都是重复的。这种方法可以将提案撰写时间从数小时缩短到几分钟，并保持提案质量的一致性。

## 6\. Customer Support Knowledge Base Plugin6. 客户支持知识库插件

**What it does:** Ingests a company's FAQ, documentation, and past support tickets. When a support question comes in, it drafts a response using the company's actual knowledge base and tone.**功能：** 导入公司的常见问题解答、文档和过往支持工单。当收到支持问题时，它会使用公司现有的知识库和语气自动生成回复。

**Who buys it:** E-commerce brands, SaaS companies, any business with a support inbox.**目标用户：** 电商企业、SaaS 公司、任何拥有客服邮箱的企业。

**How to price it:** $2,000-5,000 setup + $500/month.**定价方式：** 设置费 2,000-5,000 美元 + 每月 500 美元。

**Why it works:** Support is expensive. This doesn't replace the team, but it drafts 80% of responses so they just review and send. Cuts response time in half.**其原理：** 支持成本很高。虽然这并不能取代团队，但它可以自动撰写 80% 的回复，团队成员只需审核并发送即可。这样可以将回复时间缩短一半。

## 7\. Lead Research and Outreach Plugin7. 潜在客户研究和推广插件

**What it does:** Takes a target company or contact, researches them across LinkedIn, their website, recent news, and social media. Generates a personalized outreach message or email based on specific angles relevant to their business.**功能：** 选取目标公司或联系人，通过 LinkedIn、其网站、近期新闻和社交媒体进行调研。根据与其业务相关的特定角度，生成个性化的推广信息或电子邮件。

**Who buys it:** Sales teams, BDRs, agency owners doing outbound.**购买对象：** 销售团队、业务拓展代表、负责外呼的代理商老板。

**How to price it:** $2,000-3,000 setup + $400/month.**定价方式：** 设置费 2,000-3,000 美元 + 每月 400 美元。

**Why it works:** Personalized outreach gets 3-5x the response rate of templates. But it takes 15-20 minutes per prospect to do manually. This does it in 30 seconds.**其原理：** 个性化推广的回复率是模板的 3-5 倍。但手动操作平均需要 15-20 分钟才能联系到一位潜在客户，而这个方法只需 30 秒即可完成。

# Part 2: How to Monetize Plugins That Already Exist第二部分：如何将现有插件变现

You don't have to build everything from scratch. Anthropic has a growing library of official plugins that anyone can use. 你无需从零开始构建一切。Anthropic 拥有不断增长的官方插件库，任何人都可以使用。

**The opportunity:** Most business owners don't know they exist, let alone how to configure them.**机会在于：** 大多数企业主都不知道它们的存在，更不用说如何配置它们了。

## Option 1: Done-For-You Plugin Setup Service方案一：一站式插件安装服务

**The play:** Charge $2,000-5,000 to audit a client's business, recommend the right combination of official plugins, install and configure them, create their context files, and set up scheduled tasks.**操作流程：** 收取 2,000-5,000 美元来审核客户的业务，推荐合适的官方插件组合，安装和配置它们，创建它们的上下文文件，并设置计划任务。

This is the highest-margin option because the plugins are free. You're selling the expertise and configuration, not the software.这是利润最高的方案，因为插件是免费的。你卖的是专业知识和配置服务，而不是软件本身。

**Package it as:** "AI Employee Setup" or "Claude Business Configuration." Run it as a productized service. Same process every time, different clients.**将其打包为** “AI 员工设置”或“Claude 业务配置”。将其作为产品化服务运行。每次流程相同，客户不同。

## Option 2: Plugin Training and Workshops方案二：插件培训和研讨会

**The play:** Run a paid workshop ($200-500 per person) teaching business owners how to use specific plugins. Focus on one niche: "Claude for Real Estate Agents," "Claude for E-commerce Operators," "Claude for Consultants."**方案：** 举办付费研讨会（每人 200-500 美元），教授企业主如何使用特定插件。专注于一个细分领域：“面向房地产经纪人的 Claude”、“面向电子商务运营者的 Claude”、“面向咨询顾问的 Claude”。

Niche down hard. The more specific the workshop, the higher the perceived value.精准定位细分市场。研讨会越具体，其感知价值就越高。

**Scale it:** Record the workshop once. Sell access as a $99-199 course. Run live Q&A monthly as an upsell.**规模化推广：** 只需录制一次研讨会，即可将其作为 99-199 美元的课程出售。每月举办一次在线问答环节，作为追加销售项目。

## Option 3: Plugin + Skill Bundles方案三：插件+技能包

**The play:** Take an official plugin, pair it with custom skills you build, and sell the combination as a complete solution.**策略：** 使用官方插件，将其与你开发的自定义技能相结合，并将这种组合作为完整的解决方案出售。

**Example**: Take the Google Workspace plugin (free), add a custom meeting prep skill + a weekly report skill + a client onboarding skill. Sell the bundle as a "Consulting Operations Kit" for $3,000.**例如** ：使用免费的 Google Workspace 插件，添加自定义会议准备技能、每周报告技能和客户入职技能。将这些技能组合成一个“咨询运营工具包”，售价 3000 美元。

The official plugin does the heavy lifting. Your custom skills make it specific to the client's industry. Together they're worth far more than either piece alone.官方插件承担了大部分工作。而您的定制技能则使其更符合客户所在行业的特定需求。两者结合的价值远远超过任何一方单独使用。
