# Install These Plugins ASAP请尽快安装这些插件

## 1\. Productivity Plugin (Install First)1. 效率插件（请先安装）

This is the foundation. It gives you slash commands for task management, scheduling, and workflow automation.这是基础功能。它提供用于任务管理、日程安排和工作流程自动化的斜杠命令。

Key commands:关键命令：

- /task - Create and track tasks without leaving your conversation/task - 无需离开对话即可创建和跟踪任务
- /schedule - Block time on your calendar directly/schedule - 直接在日历上安排时间
- /workflow - Run multi-step automations you've saved/workflow - 运行您已保存的多步骤自动化流程

Install it before anything else. Everything else builds on top of this.请先安装它。其他所有操作都建立在这个基础之上。

## 2\. Marketing Plugin2. 营销插件

If you create content, this is non-negotiable. It handles drafting, repurposing, and campaign planning.如果你是内容创作者，这一点毋庸置疑。它涵盖了内容撰写、内容再利用和活动策划等各个环节。

What I use it for:我的用途：

- /draft-content - LinkedIn posts, tweets, newsletter sections/draft-content - LinkedIn 帖子、推文、新闻简报部分
- /repurpose - Turn one piece of content into 5 platform-specific versions/重新利用 - 将一篇内容转换成 5 个平台专属版本
- /campaign - Plan a full content calendar with themes and hooks/campaign - 制定包含主题和引子的完整内容日历

The output quality jumps significantly when you pair this with a voice profile file (more on that below).如果将其与语音配置文件结合使用（详见下文），输出质量将显著提高。

I use this weekly for revenue tracking and client reporting.我每周都用它来跟踪收入和生成客户报告。

# The Files That Make Cowork Actually Useful让协同办公真正发挥作用的文件

Plugins are just the start. The real power comes from context files. These are markdown files you create that tell Cowork who you are and how you work.插件只是个开始。真正的强大之处在于上下文文件。这些是你创建的 Markdown 文件，它们告诉 Cowork 你是谁以及你的工作方式。

## 5\. Create an about-me.md file5. 创建 about-me.md 文件

This is your professional identity in a text file.这是您的职业身份信息（文本文件）。

Include:包括：

- Your name, role, company您的姓名、职务、公司
- How you prefer to communicate (direct? detailed?)你喜欢哪种沟通方式（直接沟通？详细沟通？）
- Your timezone and working hours您的时区和工作时间
- 2-3 examples of your writing or work style请举2-3个您写作或工作风格的例子。

Cowork reads this before every response. No more explaining who you are.Cowork 会在每次回复前阅读此信息。无需再解释您的身份。

## 6\. Create a brand-voice.md file6. 创建 brand-voice.md 文件

If you create any content, this file is mandatory.如果您要创建任何内容，则必须包含此文件。

Include:包括：

- Words you use vs. words you avoid你使用的词语与你避免使用的词语
- Tone (casual, professional, bold, supportive)语气（随意、专业、大胆、支持）
- Example sentences that sound like you听起来像你的例句
- Topics you cover and topics you don't你涵盖的主题和你不涵盖的主题

I have a 2,000-word voice profile. Every draft sounds like me without editing.我有一份2000字的语音样本。每一份草稿未经修改，听起来都像我自己的声音。

## 7\. Create a current-projects.md file7. 创建 current-projects.md 文件

A living document of what you're working on right now.一份记录你当前工作内容的动态文档。

Include:包括：

- Active projects with status活跃项目状态
- Key deadlines this week本周重要截止日期
- Blockers or open questions障碍或未解决的问题
- Links to relevant docs or resources相关文档或资源的链接

Update this weekly. Cowork becomes an assistant who actually knows what's on your plate.每周更新。共享办公空间就像一位贴心的助手，真正了解你手头的工作。

# The Workflows That Save Real Time节省实时时间的工作流程

## 8\. Morning Dashboard Workflow8. 早晨仪表盘工作流程

Start every day with context, not chaos.每天都从清晰的思路开始，而不是混乱的局面。

Prompt: 迅速的：

```markdown
"Open Chrome and set up my morning dashboard. Open Gmail, Google
Calendar (week view), Notion, and Twitter notifications side by side. 
Summarize what's on my calendar today."
```

Takes 30 seconds instead of 10 minutes of clicking.只需 30 秒，而不是点击 10 分钟。

## 9\. Meeting Prep Workflow9. 会议准备工作流程

Never walk into a call cold again.以后再也不要贸然接听陌生来电了。

Prompt: 迅速的：

```markdown
"I have a meeting with [NAME] from [COMPANY] in 30 minutes. 
Research them: LinkedIn profile, company website, recent news, any 
email history we have. Create a one-page brief with 3 talking points."
```

This is the workflow that made me stop using ChatGPT for work. The integration with your actual tools changes everything.正是这种工作流程让我最终放弃了在工作中使用 ChatGPT。它与你们现有工具的集成彻底改变了一切。

## 10\. Content Repurposing Workflow10. 内容再利用工作流程

One piece of content becomes five.一条内容变成了五条。

Prompt: 迅速的：

```markdown
"Read [this YouTube video/podcast/article]. Create: 5 Twitter posts, 1 
LinkedIn post, 1 newsletter draft, and 10 short-form video clip ideas 
with timestamps. Save everything to Google Drive."
```

I run this every time I publish a podcast episode. Hour of work compressed into 15 minutes.我每次发布播客节目都会运行这个程序。一个小时的工作量压缩到了15分钟。

## 11\. End-of-Day Shutdown Workflow11. 日终停机工作流程

Close loops and prep for tomorrow.完成所有步骤，为明天做好准备。

Prompt: 迅速的：

```markdown
"Help me shut down for the day. Check my task manager for anything 
urgent I missed. Look at tomorrow's calendar and flag any prep I need. 
Create a sticky note on my desktop with my top 3 priorities for 
tomorrow."
```

Clean shutdown means you start tomorrow fresh instead of anxious.干净利落地关机意味着你可以精神饱满地迎接明天，而不是焦虑不安。

## 13\. The Meta-Prompt That Makes Everything Better13. 让一切都变得更美好的元提示

Before running any workflow, prime Cowork with this:在运行任何工作流程之前，请先使用以下命令初始化 Cowork：

```markdown
"You are my executive assistant. You have access to my computer and
 can control apps, browser, and files. When I give you tasks: ask 
clarifying questions if needed, narrate what you're doing, pause before 
any destructive actions (deleting, sending), save important outputs to
Google Docs for my review."
```

This single prompt prevents 90% of the mistakes people complain about.这条提示就能避免人们抱怨的90%的错误。
